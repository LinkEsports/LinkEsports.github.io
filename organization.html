What do we do?

As a brand new organisation, we at Link aren't doing as much as we would like to do yet. Regardless, we try to organise as many events as possible to ensure that you, our (future) member has enough to do. In exchange, you can help us organise more events by becoming an active member and joining one of our committees! The current committees are as follows: League of Legends, Counterstrike: Global Offensive, Financial, Social Events and Party games. Examples of events are small and large scale tournaments, viewing parties, trips to live events and LAN parties and workshops to improve your gaming skills. If you have a great idea for a new type of event we could organise, let us know!

Why join us?

Besides all these events, Link offers a platform for you to organise gaming related events, meet like minded people and grow your social circle. No matter what type of games you like, there will always be someone to talk to about your favorite game, just like there will always be someone to play with! We focus on providing a friendly and open environment for everyone interested in games, no matter what the circumstances are. We pride ourself on the social part of Link, since we believe it is specifically this which is the heart of our association.
